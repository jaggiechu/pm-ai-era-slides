What's Different from Old-School PM

Try Faster. Build Earlier. Overturn Yourself.

1

Mini prototypes, not 10-page PRDs

Get feasibility signal in hours, not weeks. Ship a rough version before writing the spec

2

Re-evaluate after every model release

Workarounds for old limitations become unnecessary complexity. Kill your darlings

3

Unlearn your priors

Yesterday's best practice → today's tech debt. Be willing to throw away your own work

Example: Meta Ads creative team — shorter cycles, everyone (eng, design, DS, UXR, PM) runs small experiments off-roadmap

Be Technical — I

Know the Boundaries of AI

Level 1: What works today vs. what doesn't
Level 2: What gets unlocked in 3-6 months — and why
→ Build where need meets capability

  • Papers: Abstract → Conclusion → main body only if curious
  • Many latest and the best stuff are NOT in papers. Follow X, Hacker News, builder communities
  • Try first, ask second. Before asking a human, ask AI.  Overcome "human righteousness"
